Critical Success Factors

Feedstock First

Secure 2-3 year battery supply agreements with collectors, electronics recyclers, or automotive OEMs before purchasing equipment.

Offtake Contracts

Identify 2-3 black mass buyers (Li-Cycle, Redwood Materials, Umicore, Glencore) and negotiate pricing tied to metal prices.

Safety Budget - Non-Negotiable

Do not reduce Western safety system allocation. One incident can destroy your business. Insurance requires proper systems.

Installation Planning

Budget 25-35% above equipment cost for installation, integration, and commissioning. Chinese equipment requires more planning.

Spare Parts Inventory

Order 6-month spare parts inventory with equipment delivery. Lead times from China are 4-8 weeks. Minimize downtime risk.
