## Critical Success Factors

1. **Feedstock Security** - Secure 2-3 year agreements with battery collectors/OEMs before investing
2. **Black Mass Offtake** - Identify 2-3 buyers (Li-Cycle, Umicore, Glencore)
3. **Regulatory Compliance** - Budget for environmental permits and hazardous waste handling
4. **Technical Expertise** - Hire experienced recycling engineers for optimization
5. **Safety Culture** - Never compromise on safety systems; one incident can destroy the business
