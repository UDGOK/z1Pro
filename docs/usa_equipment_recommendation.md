# Recommended Equipment Stack for USA Battery Recycling Plant

## Hybrid Approach: Chinese Equipment + Western Safety Systems

**Target Capacity:** 1-2 t/h (1,000-2,000 kg/h)
**Estimated CAPEX:** $500K-$1M (excluding installation)
**Payback Period:** 24-30 months

---

## Why This Mix Works

- **Chinese equipment (60-70% savings):** Shredders, crushers, screens, and air classifiers are commodity equipment with mature global manufacturing
- **Western safety (non-negotiable):** Nitrogen systems, fire suppression, and gas detection require proven reliability for insurance approval
- **Western separation (worth premium):** Eriez separators deliver measurably better recovery rates - premium recovered in 12-18 months

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Feedstock first** - Secure 2-3 year battery supply agreements before purchasing
2. **Offtake contracts** - Identify black mass buyers (Li-Cycle, Redwood, etc.)
3. **Safety budget** - Do not reduce Western safety system allocation
4. **Installation planning** - Budget 25-35% above equipment cost for installation
5. **Spare parts** - Order 6-month spare parts inventory with equipment delivery
